Our Mission & Vision

Empowering tomorrow's computing leaders through collaboration, innovation, and professional growth.

Mission

To advance computing as a science and profession by fostering innovation, collaboration, and excellence among students. We bridge the gap between academic theory and industry practice, preparing the next generation of computing leaders.

Vision

To be the premier student organization that transforms computing education through hands-on experience, industry connections, and a supportive community that empowers every member to achieve their full potential.

Values

Excellence in everything we do, inclusive collaboration that welcomes all perspectives, continuous innovation that pushes boundaries, and ethical leadership that makes a positive impact on society.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a leading force in computing education and innovation.

2020

Chapter Foundation

APEC ACM Student Chapter was officially established with 15 founding members, driven by a shared vision to create a vibrant computing community on campus.

2021

First Annual Hackathon

Organized our inaugural 48-hour hackathon with 100+ participants, establishing our reputation for hosting high-quality technical events and fostering innovation.

2022

Industry Partnerships

Forged strategic partnerships with leading tech companies, creating internship opportunities and mentorship programs that directly benefit our members.

2023

National Recognition

Received the "Outstanding Student Chapter" award from ACM, recognizing our exceptional contributions to computing education and community engagement.

2025

Innovation Hub

Today, we're 250+ members strong, leading cutting-edge research projects, hosting international speakers, and maintaining a 92% job placement rate for our graduates.

Leadership Philosophy

Our approach to developing the next generation of computing leaders through inclusive, collaborative, and innovative practices.

Inclusive Leadership

We believe that diverse perspectives drive innovation. Our leadership development programs ensure every voice is heard and valued, creating an environment where all members can thrive.

Collaborative Growth

Leadership isn't about individual achievement—it's about lifting others up. We foster collaborative environments where members learn from each other and grow together.

Innovation Focus

We encourage creative thinking and risk-taking. Our leaders are innovators who aren't afraid to challenge the status quo and explore new possibilities in computing.
